Kwanmu!

Apr 5th, 2006

My son and I have been taking karate lessons weekly for about three and half years now. And every week there are new techniques to be learned and previously-taught techniques to be relearned or improved.

My thought is to use this blog to capture some of these ideas so that I can efer back to them later. Posts will be filed under the Kwanmukan category.

Kwanmu, by the way, is Japanese for “realization of the essential.”
